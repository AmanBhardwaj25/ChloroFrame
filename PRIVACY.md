# Privacy Policy

**Effective date: 2026-06-24**

ChloroFrame ("the app") is an open-source client for self-hosted game and
desktop streaming. This policy explains what data the app handles. The short
version: **ChloroFrame does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to
us or to any third party.** We operate no servers and have no backend.

## Data we collect

**None.** ChloroFrame has no user accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no
advertising, no tracking, and no third-party tracking SDKs. We do not receive
any information about you or your use of the app.

## How the app works

ChloroFrame connects directly to a host PC that **you** run on a network **you**
control (using open-source host software such as Sunshine or Apollo). All
streaming traffic, video, audio, and input, flows directly between your Apple
device and your own host. None of it passes through us.

## Data stored on your device

The following are stored **locally on your device only** and are never
transmitted to us:

- Host connection details you enter (such as a host name or IP address).
- Pairing credentials and certificates created when you pair with your own host.
- App settings and preferences (resolution, codec, and similar options).
- Diagnostic logs the app may write locally to help you troubleshoot.

You can remove all of this at any time by deleting the app.

## Network access

The app uses your network connection solely to discover and connect to the
host(s) you specify. It does not contact any servers operated by us.

## Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children and collects no personal information from
anyone, including children.

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a
